Tutorial - Adding a Subject
===========================

.. note:: Adding a subject requires the project-level permission "Add Project Data".  See :doc:`../permissions`

#.  Login to the ReFlow server.

#.  From the ReFlow home page, navigate to the project by clicking on the project link.

#.  On the project home page, click the **Subjects** link.

    .. image:: ../images/project-home.png

#.  From the **Subjects** page, click on the **Add Subject** button located near the upper right of the page (beside the main heading). This will take you to the subject form.

    .. image:: ../images/add-subject.png

#.  On the subject form, fill out the fields. Note the **Subject Group** and **Subject Code** fields are required and a project cannot have duplicate subject codes. Once the form is completed, click the **Save** button.

    .. image:: ../images/add-subject-form.png

    .. note:: If there were validation errors with the submitted form, the form will be re-displayed along with the validation errors.

#.  Once your new subject is created you will be re-directed to the project's subject page, listing all the subjects for that project.

    .. image:: ../images/subject-form-success.png
